Frequently Asked Questions

What is boosting?

It is a process of the improving of your rank. Our booster starts to play ranking games at your account, obviously, to get wins and get to the desired league and division. We invite the best players in the world to help you to realize your dreams :)

Can booster spend my IP and RP?

They will get an access to your account, but they will never spend your IP/RP. It is a rule of being booster, if they do such a thing, we will compensate it from our pocket!

Can I choose between the roles that can booster play?

Of course, you can! You can check the booster’s main roles on the page Meet the team!

I was banned after booster played at my account, what now?

We have a strict policy to prevent bad behavior of our boosters. We choose only quality boosters. They all have passed the trial period and they know how to make our clients happy. If something like that happens, we will x2 your order, and refund you all money you spent.

Can I get a ban for being boosted?

Yes you can, but only 1 of 100 people statistically getting banned!
First ban that you can get is for 14 days.

Can I play at my account while boosting?

We strongly do not recommend you to log in at your account until the boost is completed, if you drop our booster from lobby, we will take fee's from you in amount of one game.

I have made a mistake at my order, what to do now?

Not a problem! Contact us on the online chat, and we will change any info you want. And return all money you have paid wrong.

I have paid for 5-4/4-3/3-2 divisions, and my booster jumped over one, do I have to pay more for that?

No, you do not have to pay more for that!

I can't find my question on F.A.Q, where can i ask you?

Feel free to ask us at online chat menu!

What servers you work at?

We are currently working at EUW, EUNE, US and RU servers! Check out for updates if you didn't find your server on this list

At this point the price will be forming by following principle:
Silver 5 - Silver 4 boost is count as 9€
Silver 4 - Silver 3 boost is count as 10€
Silver 3 - Silver 2 boost is count as 11€
Silver 2 - Silver 1 boost is count as 12€
Silver 1 - Gold 5 boost is count as 13€
In summary we have: 9 + 10 + 11 + 12 + 13 = 55€
